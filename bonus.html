Boston Blazers

Professional Basketball Team for the NBA

Mad Jack the Mountain Lion

To create this team, I decided on the team to be in Boston because I've always wanted to visit. We also needed to decide on a name that fit with our chosen location. Boston Blazers was the name of where my cousins went to middle school and I thought it sounded nice. As for a mascot, I decided on the mountain lion because it was the mascot of my old high school. Our team colors are black and crimson in order to reflect on our team name.

  • Our mascot was created to reflect the courage and fierce attitude of our team.
  • The current team decided on our mascot by flipping a coin: the other option was a macaroni penguin.
  • Our mascot dresses up for the upcoming holiday at every home game.
